Auto-Renewable Subscription Agreement (iOS)

Update Notice: We recently updated the “Continuous Subscription Service Agreement.” This update primarily clarifies the pricing and renewal terms and improves related wording to make rights and obligations clearer and avoid ambiguity.

Effective date of this version: February 6, 2026

Welcome to SlideSnap AI’s auto-renewable subscription service for iOS.

Auto-Renewal Overview

The auto-renewal feature is provided by Xi’an Xumao Electrical Equipment Co., Ltd. (“Company”, “we”, or “us”) for users of SlideSnap AI (“you”). When you opt in, Apple will charge your Apple ID payment method for subscription fees according to your chosen plan.

Please read this Agreement carefully and make sure you understand all terms, especially those that limit or exclude our liability. Minors should read this Agreement together with a legal guardian.

By enabling auto-renewal you confirm that you have read and agree to this Agreement. If you do not agree, do not enable auto-renewal.

Service Description

Prices may vary by region and taxes may apply. The applicable price will be shown in the App Store before you confirm your purchase.

  1. To make purchasing or renewing SlideSnap AI paid products more convenient, we offer auto-renewable subscriptions through the Apple App Store. By explicitly enabling auto-renewal, you authorize Apple to charge your Apple ID payment method to pay for subscription renewals when renewal conditions are met.
  2. This feature applies to all periodically billed subscription products offered by SlideSnap AI.

Enabling Auto-Renewal

  1. Payment will be charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.
  2. Your subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  3. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period, and the cost of the renewal will be identified.
  4. You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings (Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions).
  5. If renewal fails for reasons outside the Company’s control (e.g., App Store issues, account limits, insufficient funds), your subscription will be treated as expired and the Company may suspend service. You bear any consequences arising from failure to renew.

Promotions, Pricing and Renewal Rules

  1. The Company may, based on business needs or market conditions, adjust pricing, billing/renewal standards, or billing/renewal methods (collectively, the “Billing/Renewal Rules”). For App Store subscriptions, Apple will notify you of any price increase and you can cancel before the new price takes effect.
  2. If you obtain subscription time extension via redemption codes or other non-payment methods, the start of auto-renewal shall be deferred until one day before the extended subscription period expires. For App Store purchases, the App Store renewal rules prevail.

Canceling Auto-Renewal

  1. You can cancel auto-renewal via: Settings on your Apple device > Apple ID > Subscriptions > SlideSnap AI > Cancel Subscription.
  2. If OS updates change cancellation paths, contact Apple Support for assistance.
  3. Cancelling auto-renewal revokes your authorization for Apple to automatically charge your Apple ID, but does not affect charges already completed before cancellation took effect.
  4. Regardless of the reason you stop using a paid product, you must proactively cancel auto-renewal for that product. If you do not cancel and a charge occurs, the Company will continue service for the paid period after a successful charge.

Agreement Amendments

  1. The Company may amend this Agreement in response to internet development, legal/regulatory changes, or business needs. We will notify you of material changes by appropriate means (in-app notice, announcement, or other channels).
  2. If you do not agree to an amendment, you may disable auto-renewal. Continued use of services after notice shall constitute acceptance of the amended Agreement.

Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

  1. The formation, validity, performance, interpretation and dispute resolution of this Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the People’s Republic of China. If any provision of this Agreement conflicts with mandatory applicable law, that provision shall be interpreted or adjusted to the extent possible while maintaining the original intent; other provisions shall remain in effect.
  2. Parties shall seek to resolve disputes amicably. If negotiation fails, either party may bring a claim in a court with proper jurisdiction or pursue other remedies as provided in applicable law or as agreed between the parties.

Miscellaneous

  1. If the Company is unable to complete a charge or fulfill obligations due to force majeure or other circumstances beyond its control, we will notify users and handle the situation reasonably.
  2. This Agreement, together with other agreements such as the Company’s Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and any service-specific rules posted by SlideSnap AI, constitute the complete rules governing your rights and obligations. In case of conflicts, this Agreement shall prevail unless otherwise expressly stated in other notices.
  3. If you have questions regarding this Agreement, please contact SlideSnap AI customer support via the in-app support channel or through contact information published by the Company.
