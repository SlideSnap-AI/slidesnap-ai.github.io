SlideSnap AI — Subscription Management (iOS Only)

Prices may vary by region and taxes may apply. The applicable price will be shown in the App Store before you confirm your purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your SlideSnap subscription at any time.

To cancel your SlideSnap subscription on iOS:

  • Step 1 > Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
  • Step 2 > Tap your name (Apple ID) at the top of the screen.
  • Step 3 > Tap “Subscriptions.”
  • Step 4 > Locate the SlideSnap subscription you want to cancel and tap it.
  • Step 5 > Tap “Cancel Subscription” and confirm.

Once cancellation is confirmed, you will not be billed again. You will retain access to paid features until the end of the current billing cycle.

Important renewal notes:

  • Payment will be charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

How do I request a refund (iOS)?

For purchases made through the Apple App Store, refunds are handled by Apple. To request a refund or report a problem:

  • Visit Apple’s Report a Problem page: https://support.apple.com/contact
  • Or open the receipt email from Apple and use the “Report a Problem” link.

If you need help locating your order or you believe there is an issue we can assist with, please contact SlideSnap support at hbhuisheng@gmail.com and include your App Store receipt or order number. We will help where possible, but final refund decisions for App Store purchases are made by Apple.

Why doesn’t my subscription work on my account?

  • Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID that was used to purchase the subscription. Purchases tied to an Apple ID will not appear under a different Apple ID.
  • If you subscribed without signing into a SlideSnap account in-app (some App Store purchases can be made while logged out), link your purchase to your SlideSnap account by signing in with the same Apple ID or follow the in-app instructions to restore purchases.
  • If you restored purchases and still do not see subscription benefits, try the following:iOS: Open the SlideSnap app → Profile (or Settings) → Restore Purchases.

If problems persist, please send us a screenshot of your subscription listing (from Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions) and your SlideSnap account email to hbhuisheng@gmail.com.

Why was I charged more than I expected?

  • Check whether you purchased a monthly plan, an annual plan billed upfront, or a promotional bundle—annual or multi-month plans are charged once for the whole period and may appear as a larger single charge.
  • Review your App Store receipt for the exact amount and billing period.

If you believe the charge is incorrect, follow the refund steps above or contact hbhuisheng@gmail.com with your receipt.

What are auto‑renewable payments?

SlideSnap uses auto-renewable subscriptions on iOS. When you subscribe, Apple will automatically renew your subscription at the end of each billing period and charge the payment method linked to your Apple ID unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date. Your account will be charged within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

You can cancel at any time in your Apple ID Subscriptions settings to prevent renewal.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan on iOS?

  • Upgrade: To change to a higher-tier plan, you can subscribe to the new plan from within the app or the App Store. Depending on Apple’s billing rules, changes may take effect immediately with a prorated charge, or at the next billing cycle. Check the in-app upgrade flow for specifics.
  • Downgrade: If you switch to a lower-priced plan, the change may take effect at the next renewal date. If in doubt, cancel your current plan and subscribe to the desired plan.

I encountered errors while attempting to make a payment on iOS.

  • Ensure your Apple ID payment method is valid and has sufficient funds.
  • Restart the App Store and the SlideSnap app, then try again.
  • Sign out and back into your Apple ID if necessary.
  • If you see an App Store error, follow Apple’s guidance or contact Apple Support: https://support.apple.com/contact

If the issue appears specific to SlideSnap (app crash, restore purchases failing), please send screenshots, the device model, iOS version, and a short description to hbhuisheng@gmail.com so we can investigate.

Additional notes

  • App Store purchases are subject to Apple’s policies. SlideSnap can assist with account linking, restoring purchases, and providing receipts, but App Store billing and refunds are handled by Apple.
  • For account or subscription linking issues, use the app’s “Restore Purchases” function and contact hbhuisheng@gmail.com if you need further help.

Contact

For further assistance, email SlideSnap support: hbhuisheng@gmail.com

About — SlideSnap AI

SlideSnap AI is a mobile app designed for office and meeting scenarios to make document capture, meeting notes, and organizer-friendly note management simpler and more efficient.

Who it’s for

  • Meeting note takers: Quickly turn slides or whiteboard photos into structured minutes.
  • Business and research professionals: Collect meeting highlights and generate shareable summaries and action items.
  • Students and note enthusiasts: Organize photos and recordings from classes, lectures, or seminars into searchable study materials.

Key features and highlights

  • One-tap document creation: Capture photos or import images from your library to quickly create a “photo document” for easy organization and review.
  • Intelligent summaries: Generate clear summaries and suggested titles for images or meeting recordings so you can grasp key points quickly.
  • Speech-to-text with editing: Record meetings or voice notes and get live transcription, with the option to edit transcribed text for accuracy.
  • Multimedia aggregation: Combine images, audio, and text within a single document for convenient management and review.
  • Optional location tagging: Add capture locations to documents to organize and retrieve items by place.
  • Offline access: Documents and media are stored on your device so you can view captured content without a network connection.
  • End-to-end workflow: Capture, transcribe, analyze, and save — a smooth workflow that reduces repetitive tasks.

User experience highlights

  • Clean, intuitive interface: Quick access to create and view documents with minimal learning curve.
  • Editable transcripts: Update transcription content during or after recording to improve accuracy.
  • Automated suggestions: Save time with automatically generated summaries and recommended titles.
  • Privacy-minded design: Sensitive content can remain on-device; cloud analysis is performed only after explicit user consent.

Common use cases

  • Meeting minutes: Capture slide photos and generate meeting minutes for quick distribution.
  • Class notes: Record and transcribe lectures; combine with photo notes for comprehensive study materials.
  • Interviews / oral records: Record audio and receive editable transcripts immediately.

Getting started (for general users)

  1. Open the app and tap “Create Photo Document” or “Record Audio.”
  2. After capture, review the automatically generated summary and suggestions, and edit as needed.
  3. For deeper analysis or export, choose the “Generate Minutes” or “Export” options inside the app.
